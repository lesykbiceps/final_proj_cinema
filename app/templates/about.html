About us

We are looking forward to see you at our sessions. On our website you can buy a ticket online without getting out of bed. You can also view information about movies, screenings, schedule. We have opened to give people the pleasure of watching movies. At the premieres of the expected films, we organize events for our guests and set themed photo zones.

Our IMAX technology comes from Canada. The screen has a five-story house, 2 powerful projectors, loud sound, clear picture. Standard equipment and patented hall geometry in all IMAX halls in the world so the image and sound of each film are specially adjusted to the IMAX hall.

So, if you want to watch a movie with full immersion and enjoy it - then you should come to us. Welcome!
